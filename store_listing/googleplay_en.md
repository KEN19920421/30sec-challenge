## Full Description
Every day, a brand new creative challenge. You have exactly 30 seconds to show what you've got.

30sec Challenge is the daily video competition where creativity meets community. Each day brings a fresh theme -- record a short vertical video, submit it, and let the community decide who takes the crown.

<b>HOW IT WORKS</b>

1. Check today's challenge -- a new creative prompt drops every day
2. Record your 30-second video right in the app
3. Submit and watch the votes roll in
4. Swipe through other entries and vote for your favorites
5. Climb the leaderboard and earn your spot at the top

<b>SWIPE TO VOTE</b>
Discover amazing content with our Tinder-style voting experience. Swipe through submissions, cast your votes, and use Super Votes to give your favorites an extra boost. Every vote shapes the leaderboard.

<b>COMPETE ON LEADERBOARDS</b>
Track your ranking across four leaderboard views: Daily, Weekly, All-Time, and Friends. See where you stand, check your percentile, and compete to reach the podium. Fair ranking powered by the Wilson Score algorithm means quality always rises to the top.

<b>SPARKS & GIFTS</b>
Earn Sparks (our virtual currency) by voting, uploading videos, and logging in daily. Use Sparks to boost your submissions for more visibility, or send animated gifts to creators you love. Browse the gift catalog with Quick, Standard, and Premium tiers.

<b>BOOST YOUR SUBMISSIONS</b>
Want more eyes on your video? Use Sparks to boost your submission's visibility in the feed. Choose from Small, Medium, or Large boosts to get the attention you deserve.
